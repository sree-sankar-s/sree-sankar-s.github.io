Aerial Capture

DJI Mini 4 Pro

Sub-250g cinematic drone delivering 4K/60 HDR, omnidirectional sensing and true vertical shooting — the eye in the sky behind every Cine Drift film.

  • Sensor 1/1.3" CMOS
  • Video 4K / 60 HDR
  • Range 20 km O4
  • Weight < 249 g
Colour & Grade

DaVinci Resolve

Where raw footage becomes cinema — node-based colour grading, HDR finishing and the filmic look that defines the studio.

  • Grade Node-based
  • Pipeline HDR / Wide Gamut
  • Finish Cinematic LUTs
  • Audio Fairlight
Edit & Motion

CapCut

Rapid storytelling for social-first cuts — rhythm, pacing and motion tuned for vertical reels without losing the cinematic feel.

  • Format Vertical / 16:9
  • Speed Ramps & Slow-mo
  • Delivery Social-ready
  • Output Up to 4K
